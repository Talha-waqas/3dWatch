Heritage & Vision

Born in the Alps, Engineered for the Future

AERA was founded with a singular, uncompromising vision: to create timepieces that are not merely luxury items, but architectural monuments of chronometry. We combine the century-old watchmaking heritage of Switzerland with space-grade composite materials developed for exploration.

Our artisans assemble every single tourbillon in total isolation, ensuring zero particulate contamination. The results are instruments of absolute accuracy, designed to survive centuries, carrying your legacy forward.
